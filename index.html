I am a second-year PhD researcher from The SIRIUS Lab, GIST, supervised by Prof. Mohamed Khamis and Dr. Ilyena Hirskyj-Douglas.

My research focuses on understanding how users express, perceive, and manage identity through embodied avatar customisation in Social Virtual Reality (Social VR). Methodologically, I lean on mixed-method approaches, blending qualitative and quantitative data.

I am also interested in how social interaction in such XR media contributes to reconstruction of users’ identity, especially in the context of emerging social norms, so as to inform the design guidelines for XR systems that better support inclusive identity exploration and negotiation.

Always looking to connect for research collaborations or internship roles in XR, avatar, and digital identity!

我是来自格拉斯哥大学 The SIRIUS Lab 和 GIST 的二年级博士生，导师为 Mohamed Khamis 教授与 Ilyena Hirskyj-Douglas 博士。

我的研究重点是理解用户如何在社交虚拟现实 (Social VR) 中，通过具身化身的个性化定制来表达、感知和管理他们的数字身份的。我擅长使用于使用混合研究方法，结合定性和定量数据。

我也对XR媒介中的社交互动如何促进用户身份的重构（特别是在这些媒体中新兴社会规范背景下）很感兴趣，并希望借此为混合现实系统提供设计指南，从而更好地支持人们包容性的身份探索与协商。

欢迎对混合现实、虚拟化身以及数字身份建构感兴趣的老师们和我联系！
